Publications
 Progressive and Consistent Subword Regularization for Neural Machine Translation
Yongqi Gao, Yingfeng Luo, Qinghong Zhang, Huibo Shao, Tong Xiao, Jingbo Zhu
The 13th CCF International Conference on Natural Language Processing and Chinese Computing (NLPCC), 2024
[pdf][poster]

We propose a simple subword regularization method. During training, we progressively changes the granularity of tokenization from fine to coarse dynamically and enforces the consistency between these multi-granularity subword segmentations to effectively utilize semantic compositions inherent in words.
